Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Chocolate chip cookies are a delicious and easy dish that can create wondeful memories for everyone in the family. This recipe only requires a handful of ingredients and about an hour of baking.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cup of flour
  • 1 tsp of baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp of salt
  • 1 cup of butter
  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 3/4 cup of brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
    • 2 cups of chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°
  2. Combine the dry ingredients, omit the chocolate chips, in a bowl and mix well
  3. In a seperate bowl, combine wet ingredients in a bowl and mix well
  4. Sowly add the dry ingredients to the wet ones while stirring continuosly
  5. Once all the wet and dry ingredients are combined, stir in the chocolate chips
  6. Lightly grease/oil the pan
  7. Place small balls of dough on the pan a few inches apart to leave room for growth
  8. Bake the cookies for 9-11 minutes
