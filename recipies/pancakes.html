Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are a delicious part of any breakfast. Light, fluffy, and sweet, they will be a great start to your morning! They only take a couple minutes each to cook.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cup of flour
  • 1 tbsp of baking powder
  • 1 cup of water or milk
  • 2 tbsp of sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp of salt

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients together in a bowl
  2. Heat your pan
  3. Lightly grease or butter your pan
  4. Pour the mix onto the pan, you can decide the size
  5. Let them sit for about 2 minutes, then flip
  6. Let them sit for another 2 minutes, then place on a plate
