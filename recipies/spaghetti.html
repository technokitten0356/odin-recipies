Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is a dish I have a lot of fond memories of eating while growing up. It was one of those meals that everyone came to the table for. We always had it with a side salad and garlic bread. These additions are up to you, but if you'd like to make this delicious meal, continue redeading.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of spaghetti noodles
  • 1 16oz. jar of spaghetti sauce
  • 1/2 a cup of salt
  • 1/4 a cup of veggie oil
  • 1 tsp of pepper
  • 1 tsp of garlic powder
  • 2 tsp of onion powder
  • 1 tsp oregeno
  • 1 tsp of basil

Steps

  1. Bring an 8 quart pot, filled about halfway with water, to a boil
  2. Once you have a rolling boil going, add all but 1 tsp of salt and all of the oil to the water
  3. When the water comes back to a boil, add in your noodles and follow box directions for cook time
  4. While the noodles are cooking, bring your sauce and the rest of the ingredients to a light simmer in a seperate pan
  5. Strain the noodles once they are finished and add them to the sauce pan
  6. Plate your noodles and enjoy
